Advanced RAG: Key Techniques

Advanced RAG employs techniques to refine retrieval quality before (pre-retrieval) and after (post-retrieval) the initial search.

Pre-retrieval Strategies

Query Expansion

Augments user queries with synonyms or related terms to capture more relevant docs.

Query Rewriting

Transforms queries into more effective search forms, breaking down complex questions.

Advanced Indexing

Uses sophisticated chunking (hierarchical, semantic) and embedding models.

Post-retrieval Strategies

Re-ranking

Applies secondary models to score and prioritize retrieved docs by relevance.

Contextual Compression

Extracts key sentences from docs to fit LLM context window and reduce noise.

Key Benefits

  • Improves retrieval quality and relevance
  • Reduces noise and irrelevant information
