Future Directions in RAG Research

Exploring emerging approaches that enhance RAG capabilities.

Graph-RAG

Integrates structured knowledge graphs with RAG systems.

  • Enables navigation of complex relationships
  • Improves reasoning over structured data

Self-RAG

Incorporates self-reflection capabilities into the generation process.

  • Enables LLMs to evaluate their own responses
  • Reduces hallucination through internal verification

Multimodal-RAG

Extends RAG to integrate cross-modal information.

  • Processes text with images, audio, video
  • Enables richer context understanding

These approaches aim to enhance RAG systems' capabilities and address current limitations.
