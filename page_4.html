Motivation: Why RAG Matters

Large Language Models (LLMs) face key limitations in domain-specific and knowledge-intensive tasks. RAG addresses these challenges, enhancing LLM capabilities across all paradigms.

Solving Hallucinations

LLMs may generate inaccurate content. RAG ensures factual accuracy by grounding generation in external knowledge.

Updating Knowledge

LLMs' knowledge is limited to training data. RAG enables access to current external databases for latest information.

Improving Transparency

RAG provides traceable sources, enhancing content credibility through explicit citation of external information.

Domain Integration

RAG allows LLMs to leverage domain-specific knowledge bases, improving performance in specialized fields.
