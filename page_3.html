Introduction: The Evolution of RAG

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) has emerged as a pivotal technique for enhancing Large Language Models (LLMs), addressing their inherent limitations by integrating external knowledge sources.

Key Benefits of RAG

Enhanced Factual Accuracy

Generates more accurate responses by grounding in external evidence

Dynamic Knowledge Updates

Incorporates fresh information without retraining the model

Improved Transparency

Provides traceable sources for generated content

Evolution of RAG Paradigms

Naive RAG

Foundational approach

Advanced RAG

Enhanced techniques

Modular RAG

Flexible architecture

This presentation explores how RAG has evolved from a simple retrieval-then-read approach to sophisticated architectures, enhancing LLM capabilities through external knowledge integration.
