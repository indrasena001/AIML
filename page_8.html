Advanced RAG: Overview & Motivation

Advanced RAG emerged to address limitations of Naive RAG by enhancing both pre-retrieval and post-retrieval stages, improving the quality and relevance of context provided to LLMs.

Motivation

  • Overcome suboptimal retrieval accuracy of Naive RAG
  • Reduce noise and irrelevance passed to the generator
  • Improve factual accuracy and coherence of outputs

Key Enhancements

Pre-retrieval

Query expansion, rewriting, and advanced indexing

Post-retrieval

Re-ranking and contextual compression

Quality Focus

Prioritizing relevant information

Context Management

Optimizing information density

"Advanced RAG seeks to ensure that the LLM receives the most pertinent and high-quality information, thereby leading to more accurate, coherent, and grounded generations."
